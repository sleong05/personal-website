Bouncy Ball (Project 3)

Samuel Leong • October 16, 2025 • CS 417 • Computer Animation

Storyboards & Description

Story Summary

A tragic tale of a poor emoji falling down a flight of stairs. It begins with him rolling backwards only to realize that he has rolled off a ledge. Then, he falls while spinning with each turn revealing a face in deeper agony. Then, "Splat!" he hits the bottom...

Disney Principles of Animation

Principle 1: Squash & Stretch

The poor emoji squashes and stretches on his way down the stairs :(

Principle 2: Anticipation

The poor emoji freezes mid-air, looks around, only to realize his upcoming fall before it actually occurs.

Principle 3: Appeal

The emoji radiates exagerated emotions.
